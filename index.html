Havenwatch Foundation for Child Protection

Civilian-led, non-governmental organization focused on online child safety and prevention.

Our Structure

Havenwatch operates through specialized departments to ensure legal compliance, operational efficiency, and trauma-aware handling of online child protection cases.

Departments & Roles

  • Intake & Triage: Receives reports, verifies evidence, summarizes cases, approves/rejects, and requests additional info when necessary. Roles: Intake Reviewer, Intake Lead.
  • Digital Investigations & Analysis: Performs OSINT analysis on cases using open-source methods only. Roles: Digital Investigator, Analysis Specialist.
  • Legal, Policy & External Relations: Ensures compliance and manages lawful reporting. Roles: Field Legal Reviewer, Policy & Standards Advisor, Cybertipping & External Relations Specialist.
  • Operations & Case Management: Manages internal stability, case assignment, and volunteer access. Roles: Operations Coordinator, Case Management Officer, Internal Compliance Officer.
  • Communications, Education & Outreach: Creates educational content, researches trends, and publishes awareness materials. Roles: Content Creator, Editor & Publisher, Researcher.

Volunteer Engagement

Most roles require at least 2 hours per week. If a volunteer is inactive for 2 weeks, access is temporarily removed for operational security. Volunteers can return by requesting a task and checking in through Operations or Internal Compliance.
